Step 6: Understanding Key Concepts

Batch / Lot

A batch is a group of medicines from the same production run.

Why batches matter:

  • Track origin and manufacturer
  • Monitor expiry dates
  • Quality control
  • Traceability for recalls

Example: Lot#2024001, Manufactured 01-Jan-2024, Expires 31-Dec-2025

Location

A location is a delivery center or distribution point.

Examples:

  • Hospital in City A
  • Clinic in City B
  • Warehouse in City C

Distribution

A distribution is when medicines are sent to a location or patient.

Two types:

  • By Location: Send to delivery center
  • By Patient: Give to individual patient

Status

Tracks the progress of a distribution:

  • üü¢ Pending - Ready to be sent
  • üü° In Transit - On the way
  • üîµ Delivered - Received
  • ‚ö´ Cancelled - Not going
  • üî¥ Failed - Delivery problem

Step 9: Troubleshooting

Can't Log In?

  1. Check email spelling (case doesn't matter)
  2. Check password (case matters)
  3. Caps Lock is OFF?
  4. Try clearing browser cache
  5. Ask admin to reset password

Blank Page?

  1. Wait a few seconds (loading)
  2. Press F5 to refresh
  3. Clear browser cache (Ctrl+Shift+Delete)
  4. Try different browser

Can't See a Feature?

  1. Your role might not have permission
  2. Check Settings (if you're admin)
  3. Ask your admin
  4. Contact support

Password Forgotten?

  1. Ask your administrator
  2. They can reset your password
  3. You'll receive reset email
  4. Click link and set new password
