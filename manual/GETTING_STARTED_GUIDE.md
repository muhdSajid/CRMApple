## Step 6: Understanding Key Concepts

### Batch / Lot

A **batch** is a group of medicines from the same production run.

**Why batches matter:**

- Track origin and manufacturer
- Monitor expiry dates
- Quality control
- Traceability for recalls

**Example:** Lot#2024001, Manufactured 01-Jan-2024, Expires 31-Dec-2025

### Location

A **location** is a delivery center or distribution point.

**Examples:**

- Hospital in City A
- Clinic in City B
- Warehouse in City C

### Distribution

A **distribution** is when medicines are sent to a location or patient.

**Two types:**

- **By Location:** Send to delivery center
- **By Patient:** Give to individual patient

### Status

Tracks the progress of a distribution:

- ðŸŸ¢ **Pending** - Ready to be sent
- ðŸŸ¡ **In Transit** - On the way
- ðŸ”µ **Delivered** - Received
- âš« **Cancelled** - Not going
- ðŸ”´ **Failed** - Delivery problem
